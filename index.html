About

Apps for keeping a diary have been developed recently, which can be accessed from anywhere and means you can write whenever the mood strikes! Moreover, you don't have to worry about your privacy and security with a diary application, as password protection and other security features ensure no strangers can log on to your account. This app allows you to share your diary with your friends or keep it to yourself. Secure Secrets diary allows you to customize your entries to fit your personality and then send them straight to your e-mail account. You can add pictures through Flickr and password protect all of your entries with a secret password.

It has been such a long time since we have been able to sit down and write in their journals or diaries without the fear of parents or siblings reading it. Online secret diaries have ensured that their secret diary is full secure. This online secret diary provides privacy, while also giving you the freedom to journal on the go. This app goes wherever you do. Going on vacation any time soon?

Keeping a secret diary is healthy for you. It prevents you from bottling things up, as you are able to release stress and let out your emotions. This app makes such a personal diary much more private and also incredibly mobile. What's even better is that Secure Secret app is free.
